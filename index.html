Places Near Our Guest House

Swayambhunath Stupa

4 km away - The iconic "Monkey Temple," a UNESCO World Heritage Site with spiritual serenity and panoramic views.

Pashupatinath Temple

10.5 km away - A sacred Hindu temple complex and UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its stunning architecture.

Tribhuvan International Airport

11 km away - Nepal's main international airport, ensuring seamless travel to and from our guest house.

Boudhanath Stupa

12 km away - One of the largest stupas in the world, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a center of Tibetan Buddhism.

Chandragiri Cable Car

7 km away - Enjoy a scenic cable car ride to Chandragiri Hills with breathtaking views of the Himalayas.

Dharahara Tower, Sundhara

5 km away - A historic tower offering a glimpse into Kathmandu's rich cultural heritage.

Dashrath Stadium

4.8 km away - Nepal's national stadium, hosting exciting sports events and cultural gatherings.
